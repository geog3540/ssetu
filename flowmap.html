Bikeshare Flows within Chicago from April 2021 Data: Divvy system data, 2021

Flow Map Project File

From the map, it is visible that the downtown Chicago and lakeshore areas serve as the primary origin and destination of bikeshare. This pattern indicates that these areas could be characterized by major employment hubs, commercial, educational and recreational hotspots with bike friendly trails or lanes. The general direction of movement is primarily north-south bound along the lakeshore drive, with a few east-west corridors connecting downtown areas. The general direction reflects the urban growth pattern of Chicago, where most bike-friendly streets and transit corridors are developed. According to (Buck & Buehler, 2011), bikeshare locations near retail hubs, densely populated neighborhoods and bike lanes can attract ridership. By observing the flow patterns, it can be assumed that the areas along the lakeshore drive features with well-connected transit hubs, dense neighborhoods and tourist attractions.
