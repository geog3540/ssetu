Sanzida Rahman Setu Urban Planner

I am a graduate student in Urban and Regional Planning at the University of Iowa. Currently in my final semester, I am working as a teaching assistant in the Department of Geographical and Sustainability Sciences at UI and serving the City of Iowa City as a Planning Intern. Additionally, I am collaborating with the City of Burlington and West Burlington, IA to develop their Transportation Opportunity Plan, identifying mobility barriers and equitable solutions. With a strong focus on land use and transportation planning, I integrate geospatial technologies and coding into my practice. My background includes five years of cross-sectoral leadership experience in Bangladesh. With my professional experience and technical skills, I am committed to working towards fostering equitable and connected communities.
