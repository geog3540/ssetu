Geovisualization Projects GEOG: 3540

Family Household Ratio at Block Group Level, IA

This map represents family household density at block groups level in the State of Iowa. The purpose of this mapping project is to identify areas with highest and lowest density and the underlying factors.

Statewide School Enrollment in 2023, IA

The map depicts the total number of school enrollment (in millions) up to 10th grades across the States in 2023. The dots represent enrollment totals for individual states showing significant variation in educational participation. Enrollment ranges widely, from 3.9 millions to 38 millions, suggesting disparities in population size or education access across regions. The color gradient represents enrollment intensity, with light to darker hues corresponding to low to higher numbers of enrollment.

Median Household Income vs % Affordable Housing Units per Block Groups in Johnson County, IA

Affordable housing is a pressing urban problem that cities are dealing with.This map visualizes spatial association between low affordability of rental houses and low income communities in Johnson County, IA.

Bikeshare Flows within Chicago, IL from April 2021

The map represents the bikeshare in Chicago during April 2021, which is the movement of bikes in a bikeshare system, how they move between stations by the users. This is a helpful analysis that can help the city to identify needs, demand patterns and commuting trends.
